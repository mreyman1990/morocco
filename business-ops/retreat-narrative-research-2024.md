## 11. KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR NARRATIVE DEVELOPMENT

### Narrative Must-Haves:

1. **Authentic Founder Story**
   - Two friends who [your story]
   - Why Morocco? Why Tamraght specifically?
   - What problem are you solving for yourselves?
   - Invite others into the journey

2. **Clear Transformation Promise**
   - Not vacation, not relaxation
   - Specific outcomes: second brain, peer network, clarity
   - Use concrete language: "Build your AI-powered productivity system"
   - Frame as investment in future, not cost

3. **Exclusive Community Signal**
   - Application process (not open booking)
   - Limited to 20 people
   - Curated for founders, creators, builders
   - "Not everyone will be accepted"

4. **Co-Creation Invitation**
   - "Help us design this"
   - Interactive form asks what you need
   - Flexible content based on group
   - Participant agency in experience

5. **Emotional Resonance**
   - Address founder isolation, burnout
   - Promise belonging, understanding
   - "People who think like you"
   - Safe space for authentic sharing

### Narrative Structures to Test:

**Option A: Hero's Journey**
"We were burned out founders who discovered [transformation] in Morocco. Now we're inviting 20 ambitious builders to join us for..."

**Option B: Problem-Agitation-Solution**
"Founder life is lonely. You're surrounded by people who don't get it. Traditional retreats don't address what you actually need. That's why we're creating..."

**Option C: Invitation/Manifesto**
"We believe [core belief about founders/ADHD/productivity]. If you believe this too, join us in Morocco to..."

**Option D: Behind-the-Scenes Building**
"We're building a new kind of retreat for founders. You can help shape what this becomes. Applications open for our first cohort..."

### Language Guidelines:

**Do:**
- Use "we" (inclusive, journey together)
- Specific benefits over vague promises
- Sensory language (ocean, sunset, conversations)
- Short sentences (ADHD-friendly)
- Concrete examples ("build second brain" not "increase productivity")

**Don't:**
- Corporate speak or jargon
- Over-promise ("life-changing," "revolutionary")
- Vague benefits ("grow as a person")
- Long paragraphs
- Pushy sales language

### Psychological Triggers to Leverage:

1. **Belonging:** "Find your people"
2. **Scarcity:** "20 spots, application required"
3. **FOMO:** "First cohort, founding members"
4. **Curiosity:** "Help shape what this becomes"
5. **Transformation:** "Build your second brain in 7 days"
6. **Authenticity:** "Two friends, real story, honest journey"
